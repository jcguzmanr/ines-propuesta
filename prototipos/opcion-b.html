About

Inés is a Peruvian sculptor who started making jewellery out of the enjoyment of fabricating novel ornamental objects.

She's a self-taught goldsmith whose work evidences the close relationship that exists between sculptural work and jewellery making.

Her pieces are to be thought of as small-scale sculptures to ornament the body — to be enjoyed through touch and sight.

As a visual artist, Inés is constantly investigating new construction methods that allow her to present unconventional, imaginative pieces. Through her work, she seeks to put traditional goldsmithing materials at the service of a contemporary aesthetic.

— Inés Wiese
